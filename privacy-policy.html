Privacy Policy of Earn Challenge

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use Earn Challenge.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (securely encrypted)
  • User-generated content (videos and images)
  • Challenge participation records

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our services:

  • Account management and security
  • Challenge participation and tracking
  • Essential service communications
  • Payment processing and verification

3. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or accidental loss.

4. Information Sharing

We only share your information with third parties when:

  • Processing payments securely
  • Required by applicable laws

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Request a copy of your data

6. Cookie Policy

We use essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our platform. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes that affect your rights or our obligations.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at: support@earnchallenge.com
